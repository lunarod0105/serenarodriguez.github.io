About

I am a soon to be graduate of the University of Colorado Boulder, majoring in Information Science with a minor in Sports Media. I am passionate about User Experience Design and Graphic design. Being able to incorporating my design interactions and UX/UI development background to create better user experiences and infomation graphics if what I love to do.

For me, User Experience and graphic design incorporates more than just creating a product or image. It is about constructing a cohesive set of experiences that meets users' needs. Each product, website, app, and graphic should tell a story and creating that story is what I love to do. User Experience Design and Graphic Design are both fields where I get integrate my analytical and creative design skills to understand and address user needs.

Unstable Design Lab Logo

This graphic was created for the Unstable Design Lab at the University of Colorado Boulder. For this graphic the lab director wanted something simple but still captured the labs focus of weaving. On the top left is the Lab moto of Generating Theory and Things!

Physico Logo

The Physico Logo was made for a project in a class called desiging for creative learning. The physico app was designed to help childen and adults add a physical aspect to learning new material. The colors were chosen to give feelings for hope and stability. The center logo features a light bulb with a character doing yoga.

Colorado Football Logo

As part of my role as a recruiting assistant with the Colorado Football Team I am tasked with created different information graphics. In this graphic the lower map is showing the path to get from the Football dorms to the Football trainign facility. The left of the INFO graphic is showing two of the top awards in college Football and a CU football.

Wishes and Wellness Logo

This logo was created for a lifestyle brand. The color of sage green was chosen to express growth. The center of the logo features a star which symbolizes the wishes part of the brand name. The owner of the company wanted a simple and clean design that could be resized based off of what they would be using it for.

Produce Point Logo

Produce Point is a upcoming project for my Ubiquitous Design course. Our project is based around designing a Produce Tracker that will tell you where your food is coming from. For this logo I wanted to communicate the tracking part. Instead of doing a simple globe with two location pins. I did a globe shape but instead of countries I overlayed lettuce shapes to communicate the produce part of the project. The two pins with lines symbolize the traveling the produce does from one location to the next.
